The Project

Our team is developing a hands-free wheelchair to increase accessibility for people with muscle dysfunction. Our system uses an electroencephalogram (EEG) headset scanner to move a wheelchair using the operator's brainwaves.

Our design process includes:
  • Reading brainwave data to our system from our EEG scanner
  • Implement a machine learning algorithm to analyze brainwave data
  • Creating the electrical control system for driving wheelchair motors
  • Produce engineering documents and drawings for future reference
  • Website development for product and system showcase

Our project was developed under the supervision and guidance of Dr. Kin Fun Li, PhD, MBA, PEng, SMIEEE
